What We Offer
Everything the
Field Needs
From registration to results, Pointing Dog Collective is the all-in-one platform for serious pointing dog enthusiasts, judges, and event organizers.
🐾
Dog Registry
Register dogs with full lineage, owner details, and breed information. Search and explore the complete registry database.
🌳
Pedigree Viewer
Visualize three-generation family trees for any registered dog, tracing sire and dam lines with full ancestry detail.
🏆
Events & Trials
Browse upcoming field trials, register dogs for stakes, and manage event logistics all in one place.
📊
Live Leaderboard
Real-time standings for each event, ranked by score and stake. Filterable by event, breed, and division.
📋
Judge's Scorecard
Official digital scoring with 11 performance categories, penalty tracking, and direct leaderboard submission.
👤
Member Portal
Personal dashboard for owners, judges, and club admins to manage dogs, registrations, and results.
Our Mission
Built for the
Serious Hunter
Pointing Dog Collective was founded to preserve the heritage of pointing dog field trials and bring modern tools to the community. Whether you're a seasoned judge, a dedicated trainer, or a proud owner, this is your home.
Breeds Welcome
English Pointer, German Shorthaired Pointer, Brittany, Vizsla, Weimaraner, Irish Setter, Gordon Setter, English Setter, and more.
Official Scoring
Our digital judge's card mirrors official field trial standards with 11 performance categories and standardized penalty tracking.
Secure & Private
Your data is yours. Role-based access for owners, judges, and club admins keeps information secure and appropriate.
